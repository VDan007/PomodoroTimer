A Pomodoro Timer to help you progress

Are you tired of feeling unproductive and listless during your work day? Say hello to PomodoroPro, the ultimate productivity sidekick, brought to you by ProductivityPilot.com. This customizable Pomodoro timer is available on both desktop and mobile, and it's here to revolutionize the way you tackle your to-do list.

Inspired by the Pomodoro Technique, created by Francesco Cirillo, PomodoroPro is the ultimate tool to help you stay focused and get sh*t done. Add tasks to your list, set intervals (pomodoros), and get to it. Work continuously until the end of the pomodoro, then take a break. Repeat 3-5 times until you've crushed your to-do list.

PomodoroPro has audio notifications at the end of each timer period, and fully customizable timer intervals to suit your preference complete with beautiful themes. So, what are you waiting for? Let's get productive!
